Modal expansion of earthquake forces

\[\mathrm{p}_{\mathrm{eff}}(t)=-\mathrm{m} \iota \ddot{u}_{g}(t)\]

\[\mathbf{m} \iota=\sum_{n=1}^{N} \mathbf{s}_{n}=\sum_{n=1}^{N} \Gamma_{n} \mathbf{m} \phi_{n}\]
